Life at The Dorm

When i was at senior high school, i went to a boarding school. one of the reason i chose a boarding school is actually to seek discomfort. I do believe to actually grow in life, you need to seek discomfort, the things you actually don't like is what actually makes you a better individual. And forcing myself to live in the dorm has been one of the best desicion in my life, i have learned a lot of things and met the best people on earth. At the dorm, it just felt like we are a family.

Taking The Leap

Looking back at my days in the dorm, laughing together, having fun and stuff, sometimes i just have a thought like "can i stay in this moment forever?" but the answer is... no.
